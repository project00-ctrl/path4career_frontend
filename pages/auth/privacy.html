Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1 Introduction

Welcome to Path4Career ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our platform, including our courses, resume builder, AI Career Shield, and mentor system. Please read this privacy notice carefully to understand what we do with your information.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Platform or use our services.

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, password, phone number, and account role (Student, Job Seeker, Recruiter, Mentor).
  • Profile Data: Educational background, current job role, skills, experience, and LinkedIn profile URL.
  • Content & Submissions: Resumes uploaded to the Resume Builder or AI Career Shield, code written in our code editors, quiz responses, and messages sent via the Mentor Chat.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the Platform, such as pages visited, courses completed, progress tracking, and time spent on the site.
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information automatically collected when you access the Platform.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive for the following purposes:

  • To create, manage, and authenticate your user account.
  • To provide, operate, and maintain all Platform features (e.g., saving your progress, generating certificates).
  • To enable mentorship bookings and facilitate in-app communication.
  • To analyze platform usage and improve our content, courses, and user experience.
  • To send you administrative information, account alerts, and email notifications (if opted in).
  • To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support.

4 AI Tools & Data Processing

Path4Career utilizes advanced AI algorithms for several core features. It is important to understand how we handle data in these systems:

  • Resume Analysis: When you upload a resume to the AI Career Shield, the content is parsed by our AI models to generate safety scores and skill gaps. We do not use your personal resume data to train our foundational AI models.
  • Career Simulation: Data provided to the AI Career Simulator (e.g., current role, target year, selected skills) is processed to generate hypothetical career trajectories.
  • Chatbot Interactions: Conversations with the AI Chatbot are processed to generate responses. Please do not share highly sensitive personal information (like SSNs or financial data) in chatbot conversations.

5 Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With Mentors/Mentees: If you use the Mentor system, your name, profile information, and chat messages are visible to the user you are matched with.
  • With Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors who perform services for us (e.g., cloud hosting, email delivery, analytics).
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your data if legally required to do so to comply with applicable law, legal processes, or governmental requests.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition, your information may be transferred.

6 Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect the security of any personal information we process. This includes encryption of passwords and securing connections via HTTPS.

However, despite our safeguards, no internet transmission is 100% secure. You acknowledge that providing your personal information is at your own risk.

7 Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access & Update: You can review and change your personal data by logging into your account settings.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting support.
  • Data Export: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.

8 Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. This includes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required to keep you logged in (e.g., checking tokens in LocalStorage).
  • Preference Cookies: Used to remember your choices (e.g., dark mode settings).
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with the site to improve user experience.

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, but doing so may affect the functionality of the Platform.

9 Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

10 Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be indicated by a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
