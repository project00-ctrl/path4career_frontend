Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about building your resume with Path4Career.

Is Path4Career completely free to use?

Yes — all 90 templates, the in-browser editor, file upload, and all 4 download formats are free to use.

What file formats can I upload?

Path4Career accepts PDF (text-based), DOCX, DOC and plain TXT files. Our smart parser extracts your data automatically.

What is the difference between template tiers?

Basic (30) — clean traditional layouts. Intermediate (30) — stronger visual identity with colour accents. Premium (30) — gradient headers, dark sidebars and photo upload support.

How does inline editing work?

Click the Edit button in any template preview. Every text element becomes directly editable. Click Save when done.

Which download format should I use?

PDF for visually perfect copies, DOCX/DOC for editable Word documents, TXT for large ATS portals that strip formatting.

Can I add a professional photo?

Yes — all 30 Premium templates include a photo upload area. Click the camera placeholder to upload your headshot.

Do I need to create an account?

You can preview all templates without an account. Sign in to unlock editing, upload, and download features.
